## Overview
This dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of employee data across multiple HR metrics, focusing on workforce distribution, salary trends, job satisfaction, and employee terminations.  
The primary objective is to help the HR department identify factors affecting retention, monitor performance, and improve employee engagement through data-driven insights.  

Key metrics explored include:
- Gender distribution  
- Education level  
- Job satisfaction by headcount  
- Monthly and yearly salary trends  
- Termination rate and trends  
- Work-life balance indicators  

By visualizing workforce data, this dashboard empowers HR professionals to make informed decisions about hiring, retention, and workforce planning.

## Problem Statement
Organizations often face challenges in managing employee retention, identifying the causes of terminations, and ensuring fair compensation across gender and education levels.  
This project addresses questions such as:

- What is the overall employee distribution by gender and education level?  
- Which departments or job roles experience the highest terminations?  
- How does salary vary across time, departments, and employee types?  
- What are the main factors driving employee satisfaction and retention?  

---

## Key Steps

### 1. **Data Collection**
- Collected HR dataset containing employee details such as gender, salary, education, satisfaction, and employment status.  
- Imported raw data into **Excel** for transformation and analysis.

### 2. **Data Cleaning**
- Removed duplicates and corrected inconsistent entries.  
- Handled missing values in education and salary fields.  
- Created calculated columns for termination rate and total salary.

### 3. **Exploratory Analysis**
- Used **Pivot Tables** to analyze:
  - Employee headcount by gender and education  
  - Salary distribution by department and tenure  
  - Termination rate by month and quarter  
  - Job satisfaction levels across employees  

### 4. **Visualization & Dashboard Design**
- Developed an interactive dashboard using charts and KPIs such as:
  - **All-Time Employee Count**  
  - **Current Employee Headcount**  
  - **Total Salary Paid (USD)**  
  - **Termination Rate and Monthly Trends**  
  - **Education and Job Satisfaction Breakdown**  
- Incorporated slicers for gender, department, and year filters for dynamic analysis.
